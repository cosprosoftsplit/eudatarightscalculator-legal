Frequently Asked Questions

What data rights do I have under GDPR?

The app helps you understand your rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectification, erasure, data portability, and restriction of processing.

How does the calculator work?

Answer questions about your situation, and the app will identify which data rights apply, generate template requests, and guide you through the process of exercising your rights.

Is this legal advice?

No. This app provides informational guidance about EU data protection rights. For specific legal matters, please consult a data protection specialist or legal professional.

The App is not working properly. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the App installed
  • Restart the App
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the App

If the issue persists, please contact us at ivan@cosmicproduction.hr with a description of the problem.

How do I delete my data?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it within the App or by uninstalling the App entirely.
